Jens Olsen's clocks

The purpose of this site is to describe some of Jens Olsen's clocks, and in particular his great astronomical clock.

Jens Olsen was born in Ribe, Denmark, in 1872 and died in Copenhagen in 1945.

Jens Olsen's astronomical clock in Copenhagen (1955)

I plan to issue a new description of Jens Olsen's astronomical clock, in several installments. These installments will be made freely available, because I want to reach a larger audience in order to improve my description. Some of the already available installments may be improved during this process. The installments are written little by little, and here are the ones currently available:
